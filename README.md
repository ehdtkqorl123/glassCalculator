glassCalculator
===============

Simple calculator for Google Glass.

Optimized for XE16 as of April 17, 2014.

To use, adb sideload the apk to your Glass as any other.
Once the app is installed, "Ok glass, compute..."

### Example voice commands:
- 1 plus 1
- 1 munus 1
- 1 times 1
- 1 divided by 1
- square root of 1
- cubic root of 1
- 1 to the power of 1
- Pi
